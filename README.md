# AI Workflows & Automation

This repository documents how I use AI tools to improve operational efficiency, decision-making, and workflow execution.

---

## 🤖 AI Integration Approach

I use AI as an operational layer to:

- Reduce manual administrative workload  
- Accelerate research and decision support  
- Improve documentation quality and consistency  
- Identify risks and operational inefficiencies  
- Support scenario-based planning  

---

## ⚙️ Core AI Workflows

### 1. Research & Risk Analysis Workflow  
Tools: Perplexity + ChatGPT  

- Conduct deep research using Perplexity  
- Validate and structure findings with ChatGPT  
- Identify risks, edge cases, and gaps  
- Generate scenario-based outcomes to support decisions  

---

### 2. Executive Communication Workflow  
Tools: ChatGPT + Claude  

- Draft executive-level emails and updates  
- Refine tone for clarity and professionalism  
- Convert complex information into concise communication  

---

### 3. Documentation & Knowledge Management Workflow  
Tools: NotebookLM + ChatGPT  

- Organize large volumes of operational information  
- Summarize documents into structured formats  
- Maintain consistency across systems and records  

---

### 4. Process Improvement & Automation Workflow  
Tools: ChatGPT + Gemini  

- Identify inefficiencies in workflows  
- Generate process improvement ideas  
- Validate approaches across multiple AI models  

---

## 🧠 Outcome Focus

These workflows are designed to improve:

- Speed of execution  
- Accuracy of information  
- Quality of decision-making  
- Operational scalability
